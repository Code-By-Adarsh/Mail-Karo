# Mail Karo – Feature Provider Program

Welcome to the **Mail Karo Feature Provider Program**.

Mail Karo is an **AI-powered Email Assistant platform** that helps users write, manage, analyze, and optimize emails using intelligent automation and AI-driven features.

To accelerate innovation while maintaining quality and trust, Mail Karo runs a **governed Feature Provider ecosystem** where selected developers and teams build AI-powered features that run **exclusively inside the Mail Karo platform**.

This program is designed to benefit:
- **Users** → better, smarter email capabilities  
- **Developers** → real distribution and revenue  
- **Mail Karo** → faster, scalable AI innovation  

This is **not** a plugin marketplace, **not** co-foundership, and **not** open contribution.

---

## 1. What is a Feature Provider?

A **Feature Provider** is an independent developer or team that builds and maintains a specific **AI-powered feature** for Mail Karo’s Email Assistant.

A Feature Provider:
- Develops an AI-based or intelligence-driven email feature
- Integrates it into the Mail Karo platform
- Maintains the feature throughout its lifecycle
- Earns revenue based on real user usage

All features:
- Operate **only within Mail Karo**
- Depend on Mail Karo’s email, auth, and billing systems
- Cannot function or be sold independently

---

## 2. What Mail Karo Is (Core Identity)

Mail Karo is:
- An **AI Email Assistant**, not a generic email client
- A centralized platform providing email services to users
- The single point of user access, billing, and trust

Mail Karo owns:
- User relationships
- Platform infrastructure
- Feature distribution and access
- Final control over feature lifecycle

Feature Providers extend Mail Karo’s **AI capabilities**, they do not replace or compete with the platform.

---

## 3. What Mail Karo Provides

Mail Karo provides Feature Providers with:

- Email platform infrastructure (users, auth, email flow)
- AI feature distribution to real users
- Centralized billing and payment handling
- Revenue sharing based on feature usage
- Quality review and controlled rollout
- Brand trust and platform stability

Mail Karo acts as the **AI Email Operating Platform**.

---

## 4. Feature Provider Responsibilities

Feature Providers are responsible for:

- Building the feature according to Mail Karo standards
- Implementing AI logic, models, or automation correctly
- Lifetime maintenance and bug fixes
- Monitoring AI performance and failures
- Keeping the feature compatible with platform updates
- Providing timely fixes during active lifecycle

Feature Providers:
- Do **not** access user personal data
- Do **not** train models on user data unless explicitly permitted
- Are responsible for the behavior and output of their AI logic

---

## 5. Revenue Model (High-Level)

- All user payments are collected by Mail Karo
- Feature revenue is usage-based
- Revenue is shared between Mail Karo and the Feature Provider
- No fixed payments or guarantees are provided
- Payouts follow the official Revenue & Payout Policy

Mail Karo does not pay developers from its own funds.

---

## 6. Ownership & Control

- Mail Karo controls feature approval, exposure, and lifecycle
- Feature Providers retain authorship of their implementation
- Mail Karo retains platform-level operational rights
- Feature Providers may not claim:
  - Co-founder status
  - Partnership ownership
  - Control over platform decisions

Final authority on all features rests with Mail Karo.

---

## 7. AI Feature Governance

Because Mail Karo is an AI Email Assistant, all features must follow AI governance rules:

- No deceptive, manipulative, or harmful AI behavior
- No unauthorized data collection or training
- Clear and predictable AI output behavior
- Responsible handling of AI failures and edge cases
- Immediate action on AI-related security or trust issues

Mail Karo may suspend or disable AI features in emergencies.

---

## 8. Feature Lifecycle

Each feature follows a defined lifecycle:

- Proposed
- Beta
- Active
- Maintenance
- Deprecated
- Closed

A feature may be suspended, transferred, or closed if:
- The provider becomes inactive
- Maintenance or AI performance degrades
- Security or trust is compromised
- Platform direction or requirements change

User experience and platform stability are always prioritized.

---

## 9. What This Program Is NOT

This program is not:
- Co-foundership
- Employment or salary-based work
- Open-source contribution
- Independent plugin sales
- Guaranteed income opportunity

It is a **long-term, usage-based collaboration model** inside an AI email platform.

---

## 10. How to Become a Feature Provider

1. Review all policies in this folder
2. Submit a feature proposal aligned with Mail Karo’s AI email vision
3. Pass technical, AI, and product review
4. Get approval and onboarding
5. Build, integrate, and launch

Only approved features appear in the registry.

---

## 11. Transparency & Trust

Mail Karo believes:
- Clear rules prevent conflicts
- Governance protects users and developers
- AI systems require higher responsibility
- Sustainable ecosystems beat short-term hacks

If you want full independence, this program is not a fit.
If you want to build meaningful AI email features at scale, welcome.
