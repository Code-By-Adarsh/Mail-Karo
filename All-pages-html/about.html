About Mail Karo

Founder & Owner

Founder

Hi, I’m Adarsh Jayprakash Mishra, the founder of Mail Karo. I come from a software testing and quality-focused background, where attention to detail, usability, and user trust matter more than hype.

Over time, I noticed that writing professional emails often feels harder than it should. Not because people lack skill, but because they overthink tone, wording, and structure.

Mail Karo was created to solve that exact problem — to help people write clear, confident emails without stress, shortcuts, or privacy trade-offs.

Why Mail Karo Exists

Communication should be simple. Yet emails often become a source of anxiety, especially in professional or academic situations.

Mail Karo exists to remove that friction. It focuses on clarity, intent, and context — helping users express themselves naturally instead of sounding robotic or over-polished.

Our Philosophy

Mail Karo is built on a few simple principles:

  • Clarity is more important than complexity
  • User trust is non-negotiable
  • Privacy should be the default, not a feature
  • Small improvements compound over time

Who It’s For

Mail Karo is designed for students, professionals, founders, and anyone who wants to communicate better through email — without overthinking every sentence.
